About

We love Pizza! Our Pizza Chef is a certified pizzaiolo of the Zanolli

Piz-zra first opened its doors on 1995. Foundend by a businessman Izra whose love for food drove into making his own pizza that allowed him to try new experiments and recipes which made great discoveries with time, making us the best of the best. We use only the highest-quality ingredients, including buffalo-milk mozzarella (Mozzarella di Bufala Campana D.O.P.) and San Marzano D.O.P. tomatoes imported from Naples as well as our fresh, home-made mozzarella.
